FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is anti-tarnish jewellery?+

Anti-tarnish jewellery is treated or plated to resist discoloration and oxidation for longer than standard fashion jewellery, making it better suited for regular wear.

Will PANOOJA jewellery never tarnish?+

While highly resistant, all plated jewellery can eventually show wear depending on how it is used. We use premium finishes to ensure maximum longevity with proper care.

Can I wear the jewellery every day?+

Yes, our collections are designed specifically for everyday wear, balancing durability with modern elegance.

How should I care for the jewellery?+

To maintain the finish, avoid direct contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals. Store pieces individually in a cool, dry place when not in use.
